Helping verbs


Convention/Grammar

A helping verb is “a verb that is used with another verb to express person, number, mood, or tense”.

Words that I learnt from the story

Vibrant: Full of life, energy, and intense colours. It suggests something lively, stimulating, and exciting.
Sentence: The garden was full of vibrant flowers.

Magnificent: Grand, impressive, and awe-inspiring. It describes something exceptionally beautiful, powerful, or impressive.
Sentence: The view from the top of the mountain was magnificent.

Enchanting: Captivating and delightful in a way that seems magical. It suggests something that is mysterious, and beautiful and holds your attention completely.
Sentence: The fairy tale tells an enchanting story of a magical forest.
